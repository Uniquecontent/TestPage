Our Story

A House Founded on
Principle

Lumière was born in 2009 from a singular conviction: that the marketplace deserved a house which refused to compromise. Not in material, not in method, not in service. We set out to prove that excellence is not an aspiration — it is a standard.

Today, with over 12,000 clients across 40 markets, that conviction remains unchanged. Every piece we create is a testament to the artisans, thinkers, and visionaries who believe that lasting value is always worth the time it takes to get right.
